# Transition from Demographic Bonus to Demographic Dividend in Bangladesh

## Overview
Published research examining Bangladesh's demographic transition and the key factors required to convert a demographic bonus into a demographic dividend.

## Research Focus
- Demographic transition
- Human capital development
- Youth employment
- Economic growth
- Policy recommendations

## Methodology
- Mixed-method research
- Survey responses: 62 participants
- Expert interviews: 18 stakeholders
- Correlation and regression analysis

## Key Findings
- Working-age population growth positively impacts GDP growth.
- Human capital investment is a major driver of economic development.
- Youth employment and skills development remain critical challenges.
- Digital economy investments present significant opportunities.

## Publication
International Journal of World Economic Research (2026)
